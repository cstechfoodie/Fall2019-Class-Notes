New York Chapter Award Endowment

Our goal is to create a scholarship at Concordia in the name of the New York chapter of the Concordia University Alumni Association, and in memory of Loyola alumnus Robert G. Beauregard, BA 60. By creating the scholarship, we seek to encourage more students to apply to Concordia University.
