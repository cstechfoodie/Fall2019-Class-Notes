CONNECT TO A NAVIGATOR

And find the resources you need to succeed.

Request a navigator

Adjusting to university life can sometimes be overwhelming. The Navigator program pairs students with staff who connect you to the services and support you need to succeed.

Request a Navigator

Would you prefer support from an experienced student?

If you’re a first-year undergrad or graduate student you can connect with the Welcome Crew Student Mentors who will provide one-on-one support and guidance before the school year starts and throughout your first year.

Request a Welcome Crew student mentor

Are you a faculty or staff member who wants to help students connect to the services and support they need? 

Become a navigator (faculty and staff)
