Learning support

Learning support exists in many forms at Concordia: one-on-one tutoring, study groups, workshops, language support and more. Find the support that best suits your needs.  

Ask for academic aid from professionals & peers

Learning Specialists

Meet with a Learning Specialist to discuss how to study, write or give oral presentations.

Handouts & resources

See our handouts and resources on learning, study strategies, writing, reading, time management and online learning that complement these services.

Tutoring

Writing assistants and math assistants work one-on-one on your assignment with you. Book and appointment with a Learning Specialist or hire a peer tutor.

 

 

Study groups

Organized study groups for ECON 201 and 203. Strategic Learning (SL) sessions take place for some core courses in science and geography.  

English & French conversation

Support to acquire the knowledge and skills needed to enter the Quebec workforce. We connect students with French-language resources on campus and throughout the city.

Workshops

Each year, Student Learning Services facilitates over 100 free workshop sessions for Concordia students. They are an interactive way for you to learn study skills, develop skills you you need to succeed at university.
