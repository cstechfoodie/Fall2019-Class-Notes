Sexual Assault Resource Centre

We are here to listen, help you figure out what to do, and can accompany you to medical or police services. Call, email or stop by. Learn more about us and our awareness campaigns.

Learn more about Concordia's policy on sexual violence. 

IT TAKES ALL OF US

All faculty, staff and students must complete mandatory sexual violence awareness and prevention training.

How we can help?

Our services
We develop workshops and campaigns to make Concordia safer and offer support to all Concordia students, faculty and staff.

Workshops and Prevention Education
Our workshops provide tools and skills for a variety of situations.

Resources & Printables
Books, brochures, handouts and resources on consent, sexual harassment and support.
