Spiritual life

The Multi-faith and Spirituality Centre is a home on campus for all those who wish to celebrate the human spirit in the widest sense of the word; we are open to all students whether spiritual, secular or religious.

Our approach is pluralistic, accepting and energizing, and we encourage students to live with integrity and authenticity. Multi-faith and Spirituality Centre is a service of the Dean of Students.

Our Next Event

Exam week Chill Zone!

Come take a break from your studies with us! The MFSC will have tasty snacks, coffee/tea and several activities to help you relax and re-center during exam season! 

Take a nap in our meditation room, try meditative colouring, sculpt Play-doh, do puzzles or chat with our friendly staff. Free and open to all Concordia students, come on by anytime!

When: Tuesday, December 10th, 1pm to 4pm

Where: MFSC lounge/kitchen, 2090 Mackay Street

More info: ashely.crouch@concordia.ca
