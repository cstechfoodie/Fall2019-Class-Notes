Bike parking

Indoor parking

The Sir George Williams Campus LB garage offers indoor parking option for bicycles. Get all the details below.

Outdoor parking

Bicycle stands can be found on both campuses. Find out more on the Sustainable Transportation page.

Indoor Bike Parking  

 

Provided to encourage Concordia commuters to use active transportation methods.

The Concordia Secure Indoor Bike Parking Facility is bike racks in a locked cage area — offering a higher level of security for commuters against theft. Our goal is to provide a safer place for your bike, protected from the weather.

All Concordia members can purchase an Access Pass and register to use the facility for authorized bikes (see below). Space is limited and Passes are sold on a first-come basis.  Bike Pass registration comes with access to bike tools and lockers. Members are also eligible for a discounted membership at Le Gym, where they can use the showers.

 

Costs and dates of Bike Access Pass

Cost: 

Annual Pass     
Begins: April 1 Expires: March 31
$85 (plus tax) Flat rate regardless of the purchase date. Fee is not pro-rated.

Seasonal Pass
Begins: April 1 Expires: October 31
$75 (plus tax) Flat rate regardless of the purchase date. Fee is not pro-rated.

Note: A $20/deposit is required when picking up your pass, and refunded once the pass is returned - no later than two week past the term expiration date, otherwise the refund is lost.

What you get:

  • An access card Pass to the Indoor Bike Parking Facility.
  • Access to bike tools in the facility + small individual lockers (lock not included).
  • A discounted membership at Le Gym, where you can use the showers.

 

Obtain a Bike Parking Pass

 Please be advised that there is no longer any space remaining in the Indoor Bike Garage. Online registration will resume in 2020.

Thank you for your understanding

 

1. Online registration
Registration period starts at the beginning of each semester.
To register and purchase your Access Pass, go to Bike Parking

2. Pick up your Pass
Present your email confirmation at the Bookstop (LB bldg) and pick up your pass.

3. (Optional)  Discounted Membership at Le Gym 
Indoor Bike Parking can benefit from a reduced rate at Le Gym membership. (4 months = $40)
This will give you access to the showers, as well as access to all the Le Gym facilities.upon showing your the Indoor Bike Parking confirmation. Information on use of shower facilities and more: Le Gym.

Location of Bike Parking Facility

The Concordia Indoor Bike Park Facility is in the underground parking of the (LB) Library Building. Direct entrance to the underground parking garage is on the Mackay street side of the building..
The LB bldg main entrance is 1400 de Maisonneuve West.

The Indoor Bike Parking Facility is located at the back of the parking lot (see picture below).


 
Important Notices:

Authorized Bikes

Electric bicycles are accepted in the Indoor Bike Parking Facility ONLY if they fit the description of “Power-Assisted Cycle” from the SAAQ. In short, your bike is accepted if it has pedals and muscle power can propel it.

  • Electric scooters that do not fit the SAAQ definition are NOT authorized.
  • All gasoline-powered vehicles are strictly forbidden.
  • Unauthorized bikes found in the Indoor Bike Parking Facility will be removed.
     

Bike Pass holders not respecting the Indoor Bike Parking Agreement Terms risk having
their Bike Pass cancelled.

Concordia University is not responsible for any loss, theft or damage to the contents stored in the Indoor Bike Parking Facility, including contents stored in the lockers. Concordia University is not responsible for the replacement of any disposed contents which remain in the Indoor Bike Parking Facility past the removal of contents due dates which is at the expiration of the Pass term.

 

 

For any questions please feel free to contact bike.parking@concordia.ca.
