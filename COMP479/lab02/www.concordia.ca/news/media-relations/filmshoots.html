Filming on campus

All external individuals and organizations who wish to film on Concordia campus must obtain prior permission from the University.

POLICY ON FILMING AND PHOTOGRAPHY ON UNIVERSITY PREMISES

Journalists

If you are a news journalist looking to shoot an interview or video on campus, you must obtain prior permission by emailing your request to media.relations@concordia.ca or calling
514-848-2424, ext. 4880.

Film or Video Production Crews

Filming on campus is permitted only from the third week of June to the second week of August. Outside of that period, the University will not consider any requests. Exceptions will be considered ONLY for news journalists or current members of the Concordia community (student, faculty or staff member) who are working on an academic project.

For NON-journalists to obtain permission to film on campus, please complete the fields below and submit your request at least 30 business days prior to the anticipated film shoot.

Concordia’s primary mission is to support teaching and learning activities. For this reason, the University limits activities that could hinder the endeavours of students, faculty, and staff. Filming on campus is prohibited without prior permission.

Concordia Public Affairs reserves the right to decline any request not considered to be in the best interest of the University.
