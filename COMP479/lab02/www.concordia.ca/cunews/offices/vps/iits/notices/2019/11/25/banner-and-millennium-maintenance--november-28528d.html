Banner and Millennium maintenance: November 28

November 26, 2019

Concordia's IT department will be performing maintenance affecting Banner and Millennium between 5:25 p.m. and 6:30 p.m. on November 28, 2019.

During the maintenance period, the Banner and Millennium will be unavailable.

If you have questions about the maintenance period or service interruption, contact the Service Desk by email at help@concordia.ca or call 514-848-2424, ext. 7613 to speak with a Service Desk agent between 8:00 a.m. and 7:00 p.m. on weekdays.
