Location

We'll be pleased to serve you at our downtown office any day of the week, or at Loyola on Tuesdays!

Service hours

SGW Campus

  • Our offices are open from Monday to Friday, 10 a.m. to 5 p.m. 
  • Our walk-in clinic hours to meet with a financial aid advisor are Monday to Friday, from 1 p.m. to 4 p.m.  

Loyola Campus

  • The Loyola office is open on Tuesdays from 9 a.m. to 12 p.m. and 1 p.m to 4:30 p.m.
  • No appointment is required. The financial aid advisor is available on a walk in basis.

 

Please note that Tuesday, December 10, 2019 will be the last day that the Loyola office will be open for the Fall semester.

The office will reopen for the Winter semester on Tuesday, January 7, 2020. 

 

Appointments

  • Make one at any time by emailing fundingyoureducation@concordia.ca
