End of term performances -- Department of Contemporary Dance

Share this event:
Date & time

December 5, 2019
7:30 p.m. – 9:30 p.m.

Where

Room MB-7.265
John Molson Building
1450 Guy
Sir George Williams Campus

Cost

This event is free

Organization

Department of Contemporary Dance

Contact

dance@concordia.ca
514-848-2424, ext 4555

Calendar
Submit an event
Latest news

Second and third year Dance students present original choreographies at our end-of term performances. The pieces are presented in two different programs, on two different evenings. Each program runs approximately 70 minutes with a 15 minute intermission.

 Date  Time
 Thursday, December 5 (Program 1)  7:30 p.m.
 Friday, December 6 (Program 2)  7:30 p.m.
 Saturday, December 7 (Program 1)   7:30 p.m.
 Sunday, December 8 (Program 2) 4:00 p.m.
