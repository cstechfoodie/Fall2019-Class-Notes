Department of Art History - Speaking of Photography Series

Share this event:
Date & time

October 4, 2019 – May 14, 2020
6:30 p.m. – 8 p.m.

Where

Room EV-1.605
Engineering and Visual Arts Complex
1515 St. Catherine W.
Sir George Williams Campus

Cost

Lectures are free and open to the public

Wheelchair accessible

Yes

Speaking of Photography Lecture Series

Please note that all Speaking of Photography lectures are held on Friday evenings.

Speaking of Photography is an ongoing, annual series of public lectures on the history, theory, and practice of photography. The series is made possible by the generosity of an anonymous donor, with additional support from the Gail and Stephen A. Jarislowsky Institute for Studies in Canadian Art; the Concordia University Research Chair in Art History; Ciel Variable magazine; Château Versailles Hotel; and occasional additional sponsors.

Lectures are free and open to the public.
